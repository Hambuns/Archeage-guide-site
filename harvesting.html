Farming

Farming is the planting and harvesting of vegetables, grains and other crops. Not to be confused with gathering. Seeds for farming can be purchased either through the seed merchant, vocational shop, or found rarely in the wild. The rare drops from farming crops are as follows;

Hay Bales;

uncommonly obtained from grains such as corn, rice, oats, barley, wheat, etc.

Royal Seeds;

extremely rare, only obtained uncommonly via farming seed bundles. bundles can be of any farming "type" such as grain, vegetable, root, etc. Royal seeds are important for making larders.
